The Windows To Your Soul

By Victory Amede

Whose eyes are those? I may know
Their owner is quite happy
Full of joy like an arresting rainbow

I watch her laugh as I yelp hello
She gives her eyes a dazzling shake
As she laughs until her belly aches
The only other sounds that break are of
Hallowing winds and ocean waves

Her eyes are youthful, sparkly and deep
She has promises to keep her face so meek
She rests in her plush bed
Wild thoughts running through her head
Ready for the day break ahead
